Fried Egg Tortilla

Description

Fried egg tortillas: a fusion of Mexican and Mediterranean flavors, with warm tortillas topped by perfectly fried eggs, creating a delightful and versatile dish.
Creamy yolks and crispy edges blend harmoniously, making it a satisfying breakfast or snack with endless topping possibilities.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings and sauces (eg. salsa, avocado, cheese)

Steps

  1. Heat the tortillas
  2. Fry the eggs
  3. Assemble the Fried Egg Tortillas
  4. Add Toppings and Sauces
  5. Fold and serve
