Cornflake Corn Cake

Description

A cornflake corn cake is a delightful treat that brings together the crispy goodness of cornflakes with the natural sweetness of corn.
This scrumptious combination results in a light and flavorful cake that is sure to please both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornflakes cereal
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla exctract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Combine Dry ingredients
  3. Prepare Wet Ingredients
  4. Combine Wet and Dry ingredients
  5. Add Sweet Corn
  6. Pour Batter into the Baking dish
  7. Bake the Corn Cake
  8. Cool and Serve
